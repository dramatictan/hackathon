What is an Integrated Shield Plan (IP)?


An Integrated Shield Plan (IP) is a private health insurance plan in Singapore that complements the basic coverage provided by MediShield Life.

With an IP, you enjoy enhanced protection for hospitalisation, surgery, and certain outpatient treatments. IPs are offered by private insurers like Prudential and consist of two parts:
  • MediShield Life: The basic national health insurance for all Singaporeans and PRs.
  • Additional Private Insurance Coverage: Extra benefits such as higher claim limits, coverage for private hospitals, and pre- and post-hospitalisation treatments.

Benefits of Integrated Shield Plan (IP)

  • Provides additional coverage on top of basic Medishield Life, especially for private hopsital stays
  • Enables treatement in private hospitals or higher-class wards in public hospitals
  • Offers higher claim limits, so you can get more coverage for expensive treatments.
  • Includes outpatient treatments related to your hospital stay, like follow-ups and diagnostics.
  • You can add riders to reduce co-payments, deductibles, and out-of-pocket expenses.
  • Allows access to a broader panel of doctors and specialists, including private ones.
  • Some plans cover specific outpatient treatments, such as chemotherapy or dialysis.
  • Reduces the financial burden from unexpected medical bills and hospitalisation costs.
